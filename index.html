1. Presentation and our goal

Speed Trailer is a global adventure and trail running brand specializing in high-quality, eco-responsible gear. Known for its commitment to sustainability, Speed Trailer ensures that its products are sourced and crafted entirely in France, combining performance with a dedication to reducing environmental impact. With top-tier materials and an emphasis on ethical manufacturing, Speed Trailer supports athletes and adventurers worldwide who prioritize both quality and sustainability in their outdoor gear!
